# Tofu Shawarma Bowls  
  
*Yield: 3 servings*  
**Ingredients:**  
* 14 oz super-firm tofu, pressed and cubed  
* 1 tablespoon cornstarch  
* 1 tablespoon soy sauce  
* 1 tablespoon avocado oil  
* 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin  
* 1 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika  
* 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric  
* 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder  
* 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon  
* 1 (15-ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed  
* 3 cups cooked rice or grains  
* 3 tablespoons tahini  
* 2 tablespoons lemon juice  
* 1 garlic clove, minced  
* 3 tablespoons water  
* 1 cucumber, diced  
* 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved  
**Instructions:**  
1. **Preheat Oven:**  
    * Preheat oven to 400°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.  
2. **Season Tofu and Chickpeas:**  
    * In a bowl, toss the tofu with the cornstarch, soy sauce, and avocado oil.  
    * In a separate bowl, toss the chickpeas with a drizzle of oil and half the spices (3/4 tsp cumin, 3/4 tsp smoked paprika, 1/4 tsp turmeric, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/8 tsp cinnamon).  
    * Toss the tofu with the remaining half of the spices.  
3. **Roast:**  
    * Spread the tofu and chickpeas on the baking sheet (they can share it).  
    * Roast for 30 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.  
4. **Make Tahini Sauce:**  
    * Whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, minced garlic, and water until smooth and pourable.  
    * Season with salt to taste.  
5. **Assemble Bowls:**  
    * Divide the cooked rice or grains between bowls.  
    * Top with the roasted tofu and chickpeas, diced cucumber, and cherry tomatoes.  
    * Drizzle generously with tahini sauce.
