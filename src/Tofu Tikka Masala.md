# Tofu Tikka Masala  
  
*Yield: 4 large servings*  
**Ingredients:**  
* 1.5 tablespoon of avocado oil, separated into 3 parts  
* 1 (14-ounce) package super-firm tofu, drained and pressed (you can use extra-firm, just be sure to press it for longer)  
* 1 tablespoon cornstarch  
* 1 tablespoon soy sauce  
* 1 cup yogurt (I use nonfat greek)  
* 3 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste  
* 2 teaspoons ground cumin  
* 2 teaspoons garam masala  
* 2 teaspoons red chili powder  
* 1 teaspoon ground turmeric  
* 2 yellow onions, chopped  
* 1 teaspoon paprika  
* 1 teaspoon ground coriander  
* 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes  
* 2 cups water  
* 1 red bell pepper, cut into half strips  
* 1 green bell pepper, cut into half strips  
* 1 red onion, cut into half slices  
**Instructions:**  
1. **Prepare Tofu (This should be done well in advance, maybe while prepping the other ingredients):**  
    * Preheat the oven to 400F.  
    * Cut the pressed tofu into cubes, and transfer to a bowl  
    * Drizzle the tofu with 1/2 tablespoon oil and the soy sauce. (This will prevent the tofu from sticking to the baking sheet)  
    * Add the cornstarch to the tofu, toss to coat. (This will help crisp up the tofu)  
    * Spread the tofu out onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, and put in the oven for 30 minutes.  
2. **Prepare Tofu Marinade:**  
    * In a bowl, combine 1/2 cup yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, and 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric. If it’s a little thick, add a spoonful or two of water.   
    * Set aside for when the tofu comes out of the oven  
    * Once the tofu is out of the oven and has sat for 5-10 minutes (it’s cooled down), combine with the marinade.   
3. **Make the Tikka Masala Sauce:**  
    * In a large pot, heat 1/2 tablespoon of avocado oil  
    * Once it’s hot, add the chopped yellow onion. Sauté for 5-6 minutes, or until the onions are softer and translucent.  
    * Add 2 tablespoons of ginger-garlic paste and stir to combine.   
    * Add 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, and 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric. Stir to combine  
    * Add the crushed tomatoes and water (I like to pour the water into the crushed tomatoes can to get all the bits)  
    * Bring the pot to a boil, and simmer it on low heat for 10 minutes.   
4. **Cook the Veggies and Tofu (while you’re starting the tikka masala sauce):**  
    * In another pan, add the remaining avocado oil on medium heat  
    * Add the peppers and onions, and sauté for 5 minutes, or until the veggies have softened a little bit.   
    * Add the marinated tofu, and cook for 2 minutes  
5. **Blend the Sauce and Combine Ingredients:**  
    * After the tikka masala sauce has simmered, use an immersion blender and blend the sauce together in the pot.   
    * Once the sauce is blended, add the cooked veggies and tofu and stir to combine  
    * Mix in the remaining 1/2 cup of yogurt well.   
6. **Serve:**  
    * Serve the tofu tikka masala with rice or bread of your choice (I like whole wheat pita for extra fiber)
