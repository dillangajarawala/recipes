# Roasted Red Pepper Pasta with Tempeh  
  
*Yield: 4 servings*  
**Ingredients:**  
* 1 (14-ounce) package of silken tofu  
* 1 (12-ounce) jar of roasted red peppers  
* 3/4 cup of vegetable broth  
* 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese  
* 1 teaspoon smoked paprika  
* 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes  
* 1.5 tablespoons olive oil  
* 1 (8-ounce) package tempeh, cut into small cubes  
* 2 zucchini, diced  
* 1 (16-ounce) package of your favorite pasta (I like whole wheat penne here)  
* Red chili flakes, black pepper, and extra grated parmesan for topping  
**Instructions:**  
1. **Prepare the Sauce:**  
    * In a blender, combine the first six ingredients and blend until smooth. The sauce should not be overly runny.   
2. **Boil the Pasta:**  
    * Bring a pot of water to a boil.   
    * Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions. Drain the pasta and reserve some pasta water.   
3. **Sauté the Zucchini and Tempeh:**  
    * While the pasta water is boiling, in another pan, add the olive oil over medium heat.   
    * Add the zucchini and tempeh to the pan and sauté for 5 minutes, or until the zucchini is soft and the tempeh has some color.   
4. **Combine Ingredients:**  
    * Once the zucchini and tempeh are done, add the pasta to the pan. Then add the sauce and mix thoroughly. If it’s too thick, add some pasta water to thin it out.   
5. **Serve:**  
    * Serve the pasta topped with extra red chili flakes, some ground black pepper, and extra parmesan.
