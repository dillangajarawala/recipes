# Cajun Tempeh Rice  
  
*Yield: 3 servings*  
**Ingredients:**  
* 1.5 tablespoons avocado oil, divided  
* 1 (8-ounce) package tempeh, cut into small cubes  
* 4 teaspoons Cajun seasoning (combine smoked paprika, cumin, onion powder, garlic powder oregano, and some black pepper until it tastes good)  
* 1 large yellow onion, diced  
* 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced  
* 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced  
* 4 cloves garlic, minced  
* 3 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover, equal to 1 cup of dry rice)  
* 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, blended into a puree  
* Hot sauce, to taste (optional)  
* Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)  
**Instructions:**  
1. **Cook the Tempeh:**  
    * In a large skillet over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of avocado oil.  
    * Once hot, add the cubed tempeh and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of Cajun seasoning. Stir to coat the tempeh evenly.  
    * Cook for about 5 minutes on each side until the tempeh is crispy. Remove from the skillet and set aside.  
2. **Sauté the Vegetables:**  
    * In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of avocado oil over medium heat.  
    * Add the diced onion and cook for 5-7 minutes until the onion is lightly browned.  
    * Add the minced garlic; cook for an additional 1 minute until fragrant.  
    * Add the bell peppers and cook for an additional 5 minutes, until they are tender  
3. **Combine Ingredients:**  
    * Add the cooked rice and blended tomatoes to the skillet. Stir to combine. Then add the rest of the seasoning and stir.  
    * Return the cooked tempeh to the skillet and mix well.  
    * Add the kidney beans to the skillet. Cook for 1 minute.   
4. **Serve:**  
    * Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.  
    * Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and a dash of hot sauce if desired.
